Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know :)

Can I access and borrow resources from the university library?

Yes, you can typically access and borrow resources from the university library. You'll need your student ID or library card to check out books and other materials.

What are the available internship and job placement opportunities?

Internship and job placement opportunities vary, but you can often find them through the university's career services, job boards, or by attending career fairs and networking events.

How can I become an active member of various student clubs?

To become an active member of various student clubs, you can usually attend club meetings and events, join their social media groups, or reach out to club leaders for information on how to get involved.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues or problems on campus?

If you encounter technical issues or problems on campus, you can contact the university's IT helpdesk or support services for assistance. They can provide guidance on resolving technical issues.

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

To print a document from your laptop at HZ, you can usually connect to the university's networked printers and send your print job from your laptop. Detailed instructions are often available on the university's website or provided by the IT department.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

To scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ, you typically need to use a multifunction scanner/printer available on campus. You can scan the document and send it to your laptop by following the scanning instructions provided on the machine or by asking for assistance at the campus helpdesk.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

To buy something on the HZ web shop, you can usually log in to your student account on the university's website, navigate to the web shop section, and browse available items. You can then add the desired items to your cart and proceed to the checkout, where you'll provide payment and shipping information.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

Booking a project space in one of the wings may involve checking the university's online booking system or contacting the appropriate department. Typically, you'll log in to the booking system, select the desired wing and space, and choose a date and time for your project. Follow the on-screen instructions for booking.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Instructions for parking your car at the HZ parking lot can vary by campus and may include purchasing a parking permit, displaying it in your car, and adhering to designated parking zones. It's essential to check the specific parking guidelines provided by the university or campus security to avoid parking violations.
